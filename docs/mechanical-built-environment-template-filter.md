# Mechanical Built-Environment Template Filter

Templates from `src/aec_bench/templates/builtin/mechanical` that are most relevant to
building services, structures, and built-environment engineering.

## Strong Fit

- **a-weighting** — Building acoustics and plant-noise assessment.
- **air-changes** — Ventilation rate checks for rooms and buildings.
- **air-demand** — Compressed air demand for building/industrial services.
- **available-flow-calculation** — Fire hydrant available-flow assessment.
- **distance-attenuation** — Noise propagation for building plant and sites.
- **egress-width** — Life-safety egress width from occupant load.
- **elevation-pressure** — Static pressure in vertical building pipework.
- **friction-loss-hazen-williams** — Fire sprinkler pipe friction loss.
- **gas-load-calculation** — Building gas service demand calculation.
- **hazen-williams-friction** — Water services pipe friction loss.
- **joukowsky-pressure** — Water hammer screening in pipe systems.
- **lmtd-calculation** — Heat exchanger duty for building services.
- **minor-losses-calculation** — Fitting losses in building pipework.
- **nac-load-calculation** — Fire alarm notification circuit capacity.
- **npsh-available** — Pump suction checks for building services.
- **occupant-load** — Area-based occupant load for egress design.
- **por-aor-compliance** — Pump operating range compliance.
- **pressure-loss-calculation** — Water pipe pressure loss calculation.
- **pump-affinity-laws** — Pump performance at changed speed.
- **pump-head-calculation** — Total dynamic head for pumps.
- **pump-power-calculation** — Pump hydraulic and shaft power.
- **pump-power-efficiency** — Pump power, shaft, and motor sizing.
- **sabine-rt60** — Room reverberation time calculation.
- **spl-log-sum** — Combined plant/equipment sound level.
- **sprinkler-discharge** — Sprinkler discharge from K factor and pressure.
- **steel-critical-temp** — Structural-fire steel temperature check.
- **t-squared-hrr** — Design fire heat release rate.
- **thrust-force-calculation** — Pipe thrust restraint force calculation.
- **velocity-check** — Pipe velocity and range check.
- **vibration-transmissibility** — Equipment vibration isolation check.
- **visibility-criterion** — Smoke visibility tenability criterion.
- **water-supply-curve** — Hydrant supply curve from flow test.
- **wave-speed-calculation** — Transient hydraulic wave-speed screening.

## Borderline / Project-Dependent

- **braking-distance** — Relevant to rail/infrastructure, not typical buildings.
- **davis-resistance** — Rail rolling resistance, useful for transport projects.
- **gci-calculation** — CFD verification, relevant when building simulations use CFD.
- **mass-balance** — Useful for industrial/building process systems.
- **miner-fatigue** — Relevant to structural/mechanical fatigue cases.

- **mlss-inventory** — Activated sludge inventory.
- **nitrification-srt** — Wastewater biological treatment.
- **oxygen-requirements** — Activated sludge oxygen demand.
- **pfr-volume** — Process reactor sizing.
- **slr-calculation** — Secondary clarifier loading rate.
- **sludge-production** — Activated sludge production.
- **sor-calculation** — Clarifier overflow rate.
- **srt-calculation** — Activated sludge solids retention time.
